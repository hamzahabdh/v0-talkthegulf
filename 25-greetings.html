Greetings

Sound natural from day one. The phrases every Gulf Arabic learner needs first.

01 — Meeting Someone talkthegulf
01
السلام عليكم
As-salamu alaykum
The standard Islamic greeting. Use it everywhere — always safe.
02
وعليكم السلام
Wa alaykum as-salam
The reply to above. Never skip it.
03
هال وللا
Hala wallah
Casual, warm hi. What friends say to each other.

Simple hello. Works in any setting.

Ahlan wa sahlan
Formal welcome. Used when receiving guests.

How are you? The most common version.

How are you? Shorter and more casual.

What's new? Use with close friends.
